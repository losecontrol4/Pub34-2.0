About
PUB 34 is a retro, classic pub reminiscent of the best 1940’s Las Vegas showrooms. Your hostesses, sisters Ginny and Dottie, hope to invite you back in time with their grand piano bar, vibrant dance floor, and featured live entertainment. With a fresh, made-to-order menu filled with delicious dishes that evoke Old Hollywood, we aim to make you feel like a star each time you visit.
