About
At PUB 34– a retro, classic pub reminiscent of the best 1940’s Las Vegas showrooms– we invite you to step back in time with us. Here, you can watch live performances in our cabaret lounge, dance to your heart’s content on our dance floor, and soak in some tunes played on our grand piano.

Feel free to relax and enjoy a bite to eat or a refreshing cocktail at our beautiful Pub bar, outside on our dining patio, or in our upper level Palm Room.

With a fresh, made-to-order menu filled with delicious, pubstyle dishes that evoke the charm of Old Hollywood, we aim to make you feel like a celebrity each time you visit.

Please call for reservations and availability.
